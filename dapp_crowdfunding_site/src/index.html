Crowdfunding with Ethereum

Why crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding enables swift access to financial capital from creating startups to disaster relief. Rather than having a small number of individual contributing large amounts of capital it distributes this among many people thus contributing small amounts of capital reducing the load for each person.

Why decentralize crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding can create large capital pools where the money is collected by a third party before being distributed. During this period a large amount of trust is held with the crowdfunding platform such that they don't maliciously misuse the funds. Additionally, current crowdfunding platforms while reliable take large percentages of the fund for each succesful crowdfund. Decentralizing smart contracts we can remove the need for a third party and significantly reduce the amount of funds lost to the crowdfunding platform.
