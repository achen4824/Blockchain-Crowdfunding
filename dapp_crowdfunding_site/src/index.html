Crowdfunding with Ethereum

Why crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding enables swift access to financial capital from creating startups to disaster relief. Rather than having a small number of individual contributing large amounts of capital it distributes this among many people thus contributing small amounts of capital reducing the load for each person.

Why decentralize crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding can create large capital pools where the money is collected by a third party before being distributed. During this period a large amount of trust is placed in the crowdfunding platform that they don't maliciously misuse the funds. Additionally, while current crowdfunding platforms are reliable take large percentages of the fund for each succesful crowdfund. Decentralizing smart contracts we can remove the need for a third party and significantly reduce the amount of funds lost to the crowdfunding platform. The public ledger ensures malicious manipulation of funds on the blockchain because all transactions made are transparent. Further protection of funder intentions can be ensure through additional contracts.

After Crowdfunding

							
Smart contracts can ensure buyer security by forwarding funds into a secondary contract which can then be scrutinized by the funders to ensure and enforces further developments/progress. A major problem with crowdfunding is ensuring that guarantees made during the funding stage are upheld. Using real world information (gathered by Chainlink) such as reviews we can make the smart contract lock or refund funds if the predefined guarantees are not withheld.
